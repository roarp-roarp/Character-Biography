Background

Homer Simpson, from The Simpsons, is the main character of the series. He is a 40 year old man, father of the Simpson family. His
current family that the series shows regularly includes Marge, his wife, Bart, Lisa, and Maggie Simpson, those three being his children.
He works at the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant as a safety inspector.
