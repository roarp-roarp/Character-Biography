Personal Life

Homer Simpson's personal life.



Homer's personal life includes him and his kids. He works as a nuclear safety inspector for the Springfield Power Plant. He has a sort of
negative relationship with Bart, he refers to him as "the boy" while Bart refers to Homer as his first name. At
other times, though, they can be very happy and have a great time. It really just depends on the time of day.

  • Marge is his wife.
  • Bart, Lisa, and Maggie are his children.
