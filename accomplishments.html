Accomplishments

Homer Simpson's accomplishments.



Homer Simpson has done some great things in his life, managing to leave some viewers of the show stunned. He has superhuman
strength and endurance in some situations where he feels quite stressed, making his capabilities skyrocket.

Some of Homer's accomplishments include:

  • Winning a Grammy
  • Going to space and back
  • Winning an Academy Award
  • Saving his neighborhood many times
  • Winning a Pulitzer Prize
